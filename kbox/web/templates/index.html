How to Use kbox

Adding Songs

  1. Enter a song name in the search box and tap "Search"
  2. Tap on a search result to open the confirmation dialog
  3. Optionally adjust the pitch (semitones) if needed
  4. Tap "Add to Queue" to add the song

Queue

Your songs will appear in the queue. The currently playing song is highlighted in blue. Songs are played in the order they were added.

Pitch Adjustment

You can adjust the pitch when adding a song, or operators can adjust it during playback. Range: -12 to +12 semitones (one octave down/up).

Operator Controls

Operators can control playback (play, pause, skip) and adjust pitch during playback. Tap "Open Playback Controls" to access these features.
